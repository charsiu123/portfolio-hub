## Selected work

### Habit PWA

A local-first habit tracker designed to remain useful offline. It focuses on browser-native persistence, a simple progress ledger, and installable PWA behaviour.

**Built with:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a Service Worker.

### Study Habit

A study-planning interface with editable learning blocks, accelerated demonstration timing, and a locally stored completion history.

**Built with:** React, TypeScript, Vite, and local storage.

### Preset Mall

A client-side marketplace MVP demonstration for browsing music presets, filtering a catalogue, and following creator journeys. Purchase and upload flows are explicitly presented as mock interactions.

**Built with:** Next.js, React, TypeScript, and Tailwind.
